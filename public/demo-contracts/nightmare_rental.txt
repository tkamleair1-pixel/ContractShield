RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Lease Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2025, by and between:

LANDLORD: Apex Property Holdings LLC ("Landlord")
TENANT: [Tenant Name] ("Tenant")

PROPERTY ADDRESS: Unit 4B, 1200 Shadow Creek Drive, Austin, TX 78745

1. LEASE TERM AND AUTOMATIC RENEWAL
The initial lease term shall be twelve (12) months commencing on February 1, 2025 and ending on January 31, 2026. THIS LEASE SHALL AUTOMATICALLY RENEW for successive twelve (12) month periods unless Tenant provides written notice of termination no less than NINETY (90) DAYS prior to the expiration of the then-current term. Failure to provide timely notice shall obligate Tenant for the full renewal term. Landlord may terminate at any time with thirty (30) days notice at Landlord's sole discretion.

2. RENT AND LATE FEES
Monthly rent shall be $2,450.00 due on the 1st of each month. A late fee of $150.00 shall be assessed for any payment received after the 3rd day of the month, with an additional $50.00 per day thereafter. Landlord reserves the right to increase rent at any time during the lease term with fourteen (14) days written notice. Tenant agrees that any rent increase up to 15% shall not constitute grounds for lease termination.

3. SECURITY DEPOSIT
Tenant shall pay a security deposit of $7,350.00 (equivalent to three months' rent). Landlord may apply the security deposit to any damages, unpaid rent, cleaning fees, administrative fees, early termination fees, or any other charges at Landlord's sole discretion. The security deposit, less any deductions, shall be returned within ONE HUNDRED AND TWENTY (120) days after lease termination. Landlord is not required to provide an itemized list of deductions.

4. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS
Tenant shall be responsible for ALL maintenance and repairs to the property, including but not limited to plumbing, electrical, HVAC systems, appliances, and structural repairs, regardless of cause. Tenant must use Landlord's designated contractors for any repair work, at Tenant's expense. Any repair exceeding $50.00 requires prior written approval from Landlord within a response window of thirty (30) business days. Failure to maintain the property will result in automatic forfeiture of the entire security deposit.

5. PROPERTY ACCESS AND INSPECTIONS
Landlord and Landlord's agents shall have the right to enter the premises at any time, with or without notice, for any purpose including but not limited to inspections, showings to prospective tenants or buyers, maintenance, and photography. Tenant agrees to the installation of smart lock systems that provide Landlord with remote access. Landlord may conduct inspections of Tenant's personal belongings to ensure compliance with community standards.

6. PET POLICY
No pets of any kind are permitted, including emotional support animals and service animals. Violation of this policy shall result in immediate lease termination and a penalty of $5,000.00. Landlord reserves the right to enter the premises without notice to verify compliance with this provision.

7. SUBLETTING AND GUESTS
Tenant may NOT sublet or assign this lease under any circumstances. Overnight guests are limited to three (3) consecutive nights per month. Tenant must register all overnight guests with the Landlord's office 48 hours in advance. Unregistered guests may result in fines of $200.00 per occurrence.

8. LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION
Tenant shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Landlord from any and all claims, damages, losses, and expenses, including attorney's fees, arising from Tenant's use of the premises or the acts of Tenant's guests, regardless of Landlord's negligence or fault. Landlord shall have no liability for any personal injury, property damage, or loss occurring on the premises, even if caused by Landlord's negligence or failure to maintain the property.

9. EARLY TERMINATION
Early termination by Tenant requires payment of a fee equal to FOUR (4) months' rent plus forfeiture of the entire security deposit. Landlord may terminate the lease immediately for any violation, real or perceived, without opportunity to cure. Upon Landlord-initiated termination, Tenant shall have forty-eight (48) hours to vacate the premises.

10. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
All disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration administered by an arbitrator selected solely by the Landlord. Tenant waives the right to a jury trial, class action participation, and any right to appeal the arbitration decision. Tenant shall be responsible for all arbitration costs regardless of outcome. This agreement shall be governed by the laws most favorable to the Landlord.

11. UTILITIES AND SERVICES
Tenant shall be responsible for all utilities. Additionally, Tenant shall pay a mandatory monthly fee of $200.00 for "community amenities" which Landlord may modify or discontinue at any time without reduction in fees. Tenant shall also pay $75.00 monthly for mandatory renter's insurance provided by Landlord's designated insurer.

12. MODIFICATIONS AND SEVERABILITY
Landlord may modify any terms of this lease at any time with seven (7) days email notice. Tenant's continued occupancy shall constitute acceptance of any modifications. If any provision is found to be unenforceable, all remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

TENANT ACKNOWLEDGES HAVING READ AND UNDERSTOOD ALL TERMS OF THIS AGREEMENT.
