RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Lease Agreement is entered into on January 1, 2024, between PROPERTY HOLDINGS LLC (Landlord) and Tenant.

1. PREMISES: 123 Main St, Apt 4B
2. TERM: 12 months beginning January 1, 2024
3. RENT: $2,500 per month, due on the 1st. Late fee of $150 applied immediately on the 2nd.
4. SECURITY DEPOSIT: $5,000. Tenant shall forfeit entire security deposit if lease is terminated before the end of the term for any reason, including but not limited to job relocation, health issues, or uninhabitable conditions.
5. LANDLORD ACCESS: Landlord reserves the right to enter the premises at any time without notice for inspections, repairs, or showings.
6. REPAIRS: Tenant is responsible for ALL repairs and maintenance, including structural, plumbing, electrical, and HVAC systems, regardless of cause.
7. TERMINATION: Tenant must provide 90 days written notice to vacate. Landlord may terminate with 15 days notice for any reason.
8. RENT INCREASE: Rent will automatically increase by 20% upon renewal. Landlord reserves right to increase rent at any time with 30 days notice.
9. GUESTS: No overnight guests permitted without written approval. No subletting under any circumstances.
10. PETS: Absolutely no pets. Violation results in immediate eviction and forfeiture of deposit.
11. MODIFICATIONS: Landlord may modify this agreement at any time. Continued occupancy constitutes acceptance.
12. DISPUTES: Tenant waives right to jury trial and agrees to binding arbitration with arbitrator selected by Landlord.

By signing below, Tenant acknowledges reading and agreeing to all terms.
